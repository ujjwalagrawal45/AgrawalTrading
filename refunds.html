Refunds and Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Policy

Clients may request a cancellation of IT services at any time. Cancellations must be made in writing via email to support@agrawaltraders.com. If a project is cancelled after work has commenced, the client will be billed for the pro-rated hours completed up to the point of cancellation.

Refund Policy

Due to the nature of IT services, consulting, and custom software development, we do not offer full refunds once work has been delivered and accepted.

  • Retainers and Deposits: Upfront deposits to secure development time or consulting resources are non-refundable.
  • Milestone Payments: If a project is terminated early, any pre-paid amounts for unstarted future milestones will be refunded within 7-10 business days.
  • Service Outages: For managed hosting or maintenance services, compensation for downtime will be handled strictly according to the Service Level Agreement (SLA) provided during onboarding.
