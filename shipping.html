Shipping and Delivery Policy

Digital Delivery

As an IT services provider, agrawal traders primarily delivers digital goods, code repositories, system architectures, and consulting reports. Therefore, standard physical shipping policies generally do not apply.

Delivery Methods

All digital assets, software, and deliverables will be transmitted electronically. Methods of delivery include, but are not limited to:

  • Secure cloud storage links
  • Direct code commits to Git repositories (e.g., GitHub, GitLab)
  • Direct deployment to client server infrastructure
  • Email attachments for documentation

Delivery Timelines

Delivery dates and milestones are highly customized and will be explicitly defined in your project agreement or Statement of Work (SOW). If there are delays in development or delivery, our team will communicate revised timelines promptly.

Physical Hardware

In the rare event that physical IT hardware is procured and shipped on behalf of a client, shipping costs, tracking information, and risk of loss will be transferred to the client upon dispatch from our facility or the original vendor.
